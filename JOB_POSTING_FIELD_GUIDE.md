# Job Posting Field Guide
**Required vs Optional Fields**

---

## âœ… REQUIRED FIELDS (Must fill in to save)

### Basic Info
- **Job Title** - min 3 characters, max 80 characters

### Core Job Info
- **Seniority Level** - Select: Intern, Junior, Mid, Senior, Lead, Manager, Director, or Executive
- **Department** - min 2 characters (e.g., "Engineering", "Marketing", "Sales")
- **Work Arrangement** - Select: On-site, Hybrid, or Remote
- **Location** - City, Province, Country
- **Summary** - min 20 characters (2-4 line summary)
- **Responsibilities** - At least 5 items
- **Required Skills** - At least 5 skills with level and priority
- **Qualifications** - At least 1 item
- **Experience Requirements** - At least 1 item

### Compensation
- **Employment Type** - Select: Full-time, Part-time, Contract, Internship, Temporary
- **Salary Range** - If provided, must have both min AND max (min < max)

### Application Info
- **Application Method** - Select: in-app or external
- **Closing Date** - Date when applications close (format: yyyy-mm-dd)

### Company Details
- **Company Name** - min 2 characters
- **Contact Email** - Valid email address

### Compliance
- **Right to Work** - Select: Citizen/PR, Work Permit, or Not eligible
- **POPIA Consent Required** - Yes/No checkbox
- **Background Checks Consent** - Yes/No checkbox

## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips

1. **Start with required fields first** - This ensures you can save your draft
2. **Draft status is your friend** - Save as Draft while filling in optional fields
3. **External URL format** - Must include `https://` or `http://`
4. **SEO comes last** - Fill in all job details first, then generate SEO metadata
